About Us

Welcome to Erico Pharmaceuticals Co. Ltd. Learn more about our journey and vision.

Our Story

Erico Pharmaceuticals Company Limited was established in September 9th, 2016. Our journey began with the operation of community pharmacies, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic when the availability of pharmaceutical end products was severely impacted in the year 2020

The company was founded by Mr. Eric Effah and Mrs. Adelaide Effah, who have extensive experience in pharmaceutical distribution.

We aspire to become a prominent force in pharmaceutical distribution, expand our network of community pharmacies, and ultimately transform into a global leader in pharmaceutical manufacturing.
