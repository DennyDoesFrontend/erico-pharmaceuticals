About Us

Welcome to Erico Pharmaceuticals Co. Ltd. Learn more about our journey and vision.

Our Story

Erico Pharmaceuticals Co. Ltd was registered on 9th September 2016. We started community pharmacies in 2020 when we realized the COVID-19 pandemic had become a global problem, and people in our community needed access to medications.

The company was established by Mr. Eric Effah and Mrs. Adelaide Effah. Our headquarters are located in Osu Accra, with several branches.

We are striving to become a major pharmaceutical manufacturing company, distributor, and importer in the industry. Our excellent and experienced staff work tirelessly to help the company grow.
